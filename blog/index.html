Conclusion

Throughout this series, different regularized forms of linear regression have been examined as tools to overcome the tendency to overfit training data of the Ordinary Least Squares model. The Elastic Net‚Ää‚Äî‚Äädue to its balance between regularization varieties‚Ää‚Äî‚Äätends to be the most robust to aid the overfitting issue, however, the Lasso certainly can prove helpful in many situations due to its automated feature selection.
